# Local Studio v0.1.28

🤖 **AI genre detection** — drop a mix and the app suggests the genre it hears, so you start from the right mastering preset without guessing.

### New — AI detected genre
When you load a track, a suggestion appears above the genre picker:

> 🤖 AI detected genre: **EDM / Electronic** · 78%

- It listens to the **spectral balance, dynamics (crest), stereo width and tilt** and matches them against each genre's typical profile.
- One click on **Apply** sets that genre (and its preset); or ignore it and pick your own — it's a suggestion, not a decision, and it shows its confidence honestly.
- Runs as part of the existing analysis, so there's no extra wait.

This is the first half of the AI mastering work; **AI stem mastering** (separating drums/bass/vocals/other and balancing them) is coming next.

### Unchanged
- EQ automation lanes, manual multiband, adaptive EQ, Pro parametric EQ, auto mode, A/B + three-way comparison, and download all work as before.
